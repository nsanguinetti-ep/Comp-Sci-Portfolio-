What is Vindinium?

Vindinium is a turn based game that revolves around stealing mines and accumulating more gold than the other players. Each turn, a given player is rewarded one gold per number of mines they are currently in possesion of. To aquire mines, players can either steal a free mine on the board, or the mine of another players. Players can also kill other players to aquire the mines of the player they killed. When a players health is low, there are taverns on the map where they can heal themselves for the price of two gold. By the end of 1200 turns, the player with the most gold is declaired the winner.

What makes Vindinium interesting and unique is the fact that the players themselves are not controlled by people, yet AI that the players coded themselves! The purpose of Vindinium is to code the best bot AI using javacriptt, and win the most games until your bot eventually makes it too the top of the leaderboard. This can be acheived by programing different stratagies for a given bot, such as fleeing to a tavern when the bots health has reached a certain threshold, another stratagy that could be programed into a bot to improve it's ability during rounds is too attack the player who is in possesion of the most mines on the feild. Due to the constantly changing map the bots play on, it is hard to program a bot that consistantly wins under any condition.
The bots AI revolves around the conditions of the game and the situation it is in. One example of this is having a bot steal mines. For my bot in particular, it steals mines when the task is equal to "steal mines". function only runs when there are two mines left on the feild. This sort of stratagy can really affect the outcome of the game!
